Red Sauce with Meat

Description

This is a simple recipe for a pasta tomato sauce with meat that I have developed over the years. It is very simple and can be a quick and easy dinner to make. You can use whatever kind of meat you want; I prefer to use ground beef and Italian sausage however, anything can work. I have even used chicken in a pinch. If you want a vegetarian option you can forego the meat entirely and add other vegetables such as zucchini!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 medium onion (diced)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 pounds ground beef/pork
  • 1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat up the oil in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the oinon and sautee untill soft and translucent.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add the ground beef and salt and pepper to taste. Brown beef all over. Don't worry if it's undercooked we wll finish cooking it in the sauce.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, worcestershire sauce, and red pepper flakes. Stir untill well combined.
  6. Simmer on low for 10 minutes, stiring occasionally.* Add salt and pepper to taste and serve!
  7. *If you have time I suggest simmering for 20-30 minutes to help everything meld together.

Serve this on whatever your favorite pasta or noodle is! This can also be cooked ahead of time and frozen for later. It keeps fairly well in the freezer for a month or two. When ready to use it, just place it in the fridge the night before and reheat in a pot.
